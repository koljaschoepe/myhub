# Target audience

## Primary: Privacy-conscious knowledge workers who already pay for Claude

**Who.** Researchers, writers, scientists, lawyers, consultants, founders. People with sensitive material — drafts, contracts, client data, thesis chapters — who already have a Claude Code Pro or Max subscription for coding/writing assistance and want a non-cloud workspace for the rest of their work.

**Job-to-be-done.** "I want to use Claude to help with my real work without my real work ever sitting on someone else's server."

**What they have today.** Claude.ai chat in a browser (everything goes to Anthropic), or Cursor/VS Code with Claude (great for code, useless for PDFs/notes/spreadsheets), or Notion AI (every document indexed by a third party).

**What they want.** A single app that opens their files, runs Claude on them locally, and leaves no trace.

## Primary: Mobile / multi-device professionals

**Who.** People who switch between work laptop, home desktop, and a borrowed machine on the road. People who travel with a tablet but need a real workspace at the destination.

**Job-to-be-done.** "I want my workspace to be a thing I can put in my pocket."

**What they have today.** iCloud / Dropbox sync (slow, requires login, leaves trails), or one machine they can't leave behind.

**What they want.** Plug the drive in. Be home.

## Secondary: Open-source / privacy advocates

**Who.** People who would happily pay $179 once for a curated, transparent, auditable workspace appliance because they're tired of subscription SaaS.

**Job-to-be-done.** "Sell me a workspace I own."

**What they want.** Source code visible. No telemetry. SKU-A pilot in mind.

## Non-users (people we are not for)

- **Teams that need shared workspaces.** Arasul is single-user. Sync is by carrying the drive.
- **People who want zero-config cloud convenience.** Arasul demands you plug a drive in. That's a feature.
- **People who don't have a Claude subscription and refuse to get one.** The default path requires it. (Power users can paste an API key, but that defeats the cost story.)
- **Heavy multi-modal users (video, image generation).** Out of scope for v1; Claude is the only model on Day 1.
