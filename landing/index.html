How it works

1

Plug in

Pop the Arasul SSD into any modern computer's USB-C port. The app opens automatically once it's set up.

2

Unlock

Type your passphrase. The drive decrypts your Anthropic login and your AI remembers who you are and what you're working on.

3

Work

Notes. Projects. Chat with Claude, grounded in your own files. A real terminal for when you need one.

4

Eject

Pop out the drive and everything goes with you. The host computer keeps no trace. Plug into a friend's laptop and you're home.

Built for privacy by physics, not policy.

Most "private" tools ask you to trust their cloud. Arasul doesn't have one. Your files live on a drive. Your passphrase never leaves the drive. Your AI conversations stay with you.

  • Argon2id passphrase-derived key · XChaCha20-Poly1305 AEAD
  • Cross-platform: macOS, Windows, Linux
  • No account, no cloud sync, no telemetry by default
  • Open source (MIT) · audit the vault yourself
