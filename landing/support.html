Support

Short path: support@arasul.dev. Average first-response time during the beta: under 12 hours. Below: common questions, answered.

Installing

I plugged in the drive and nothing happened.

Auto-launch only works after you've enabled it on this specific computer (Settings → Auto-launch). On a fresh computer, open the drive in Finder/Explorer/Files and double-click the Arasul app/executable.

macOS says "Arasul cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified."

Until the Developer ID signing pipeline is fully rolled out (Phase 5.1), you may need to right-click the app, choose Open, and confirm. After the v1.0 public release, signing is automatic.

Windows SmartScreen flags the installer.

Same as macOS — we're waiting on an EV code-signing cert (2-3 week lead time). Until then click "More info → Run anyway." You can verify the SHA-256 against feed.json.

Which USB-C SSD should I use?

Any modern USB-C SSD works. We test Samsung T7 Shield, SanDisk Extreme Pro, and Crucial X9 Pro. Size: 256 GB is plenty for notes + projects + Claude binaries. 1 TB is comfortable.

Using

I forgot my passphrase.

The vault cannot be recovered without it — that's the design. Delete .boot/vault.enc and .boot/kdf.salt from the drive; Arasul will treat it as a fresh install and walk you through onboarding again. Your files in content/ are not affected.

How do I back up the drive?

Copy the whole drive to any backup target (Time Machine, rsync, cloud backup) — the vault is encrypted, so cloud backups don't leak credentials. Restoring: copy back, re-plug, unlock normally.

Can I use Arasul without Claude?

Yes. The editor, tree, and file management work without ever connecting Claude. Skip the Claude login step during onboarding and Arasul is a (nicely integrated) portable markdown editor.

Bugs + security

I found a bug.

Open an issue at github.com/arasul/arasul. If you're in the beta, post in Discord #bug-reports — we triage daily. Urgent: email support@arasul.dev.

I found a security issue.

Email security@arasul.dev directly — please don't open a public issue. We acknowledge within 24 hours and ship a fix within 7 days for critical issues. We're happy to credit you in the release notes if you'd like.

Refunds + returns (SKU A)

How do returns work on the preloaded SSD?

30-day no-questions refund, EU-wide, shipping at our cost. Keep the drive, we'll re-image it for the next batch. Send a note to support@arasul.dev.
